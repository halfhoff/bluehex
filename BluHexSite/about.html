Who we are

Bluhex is a small company based in Minneapolis and dedicated to the users wellbeing. We spend our time and creativity building the Chrome extension to keep you from posting unneeded information. Our goal is to create an easy to use extension with a number of user friendly settings to make sure that you don’t lose friends from posting without thinking. We know that everyone has been there, a time where you commented on a post or shared something not thinking about what the outcome would be. Bluhex is dedicated to changing that feeling and create a fun cheeky way to do so.
Bluhex is always welcome to suggestions as we grow. Click here if you have an idea. Let’s work together to lessen the amount of junk on your newsfeed.
