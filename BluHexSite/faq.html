General Questions

What is BluHex?

Google Chrome extension that limits exposure to Social Media by disabling the users ability to post comments and status updates. We exist to help facilitate a healthy and happy form of Social Media detox. Our product uses Google Chrome framework to target Social Media Websites such as: Facebook, Twitter, and Youtube. Our code blocks the comment area allowing users to ignore pointless debates and toxic arguments.

Why use Bluhex?

We offer a product to increase productivity and quality of life. While many other Social Media blockers block users entirely from using their Facebook or Twitter, we believe that it is important to stay connected with pertinent information and to network on these platforms.

Can I trust Bluhex?

Bluhex uses Google Chrome to separate our customer credentials. We never use sell or spread your information to 3rd parties. EVER! We encourage managers and executives to have their teams install this extension. We encourage everyone to check the rules at their workplace concerning social media.

Do I need a credit card?

No. Our application is 100% free to use. We graciously accept donations but equally love when you share your experience of our product with your friends. Check out our DONATE page. Our donate button will bring you to an external Paypal link.

Do you share my location?

GPS features relay directly through your browser. While we do not track your information, your ISP will have access to places you have been incase of security issues. (see Location Privacy Protection Act) You can disable GPS at any time.

Can I customize Bluhex?

Yes, our customizable features include: GPS, ON/OFF, Timer, and Keyword Blocker

Installation

How do I install BluHex?

To install the extension visit the Google Chrome Web Store and Search for "Bluhex". Check out our How To page for detailed instructions.

What desktop browsers do you support?

Google Chrome
A Safari extnesion will be released soon! Stay up to date with the releases on our Twitter.

Can I use this on my phone?

Not yet! Currently, Chrome does not support extensions in their mobile browser. We at BluHex have been hard at work developing this extension into an App that you can use on your mobile device. We would love to hear any suggestions you have! Send us an email and we will add it to our list!

Reporting Issues

What should I do if something does not work?

First, try refreshing the page and check that you have the correct URL. If that doesn't solve the problem, we may be experiencing some issues on our end. You can email Extbluhex@gmail.com

How do I get in contact with BluHex?

Check out our official Bluhex Twitter and Facebook pages! For any additional questions or issues (including issues related to abuse, inappropriate content, spam, or if you are a journalist or analyst seeking information), fill out the form on the contact page and we'll be happy to help.

What mobile browsers do you support?

We are working with Chrome for Mobile BETA to incorporate mobile in the future.

Performance

How much bandwidth does BluHex use?

Less than 100kb average.

Can I change when Bluehex turns on and off?

Yes! Our timing feature lets you specify the hours and days of the week in which the application runs.

Can I use Bluhex on my mobile device?

No, however we are currently developing our product on Chrome Beta for Mobile.

Can I use more advanced search terms?

Yes you can! Our auto complete function uses the most popular phrases and words. You can type in any single word or group of words to customize your own settings.

How do I deactivate Bluhex?

It's as simple as right clicking the icon in your Google Chrome extension bar and selecting "remove from chrome" To deactivate your account, head to your Settings page and click 'Deactivate my account.'
