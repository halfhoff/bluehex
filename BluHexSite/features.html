Features

  1. On/Off Switch

    2. Turn BluHex on and off whenever you please!

  2. Timer

    3. The timer allows you to set a time limit to your Bluhex session. You will not be able to turn it off before the timer is up.

  3. Password

    4. Set a password so anyone sharing your computer can't make a fool of themselves!

  4. Time of Day

    5. Are you more likely to pick fights at night? Turn on Time of Day to prevent this!

  5. GPS

    6. Do you work from a coffee shop and want to make sure you're not tempted while you are there?

  6. Word Filter

    7. Tired of seeing the mannequin challenge? Add it to the word filter.
